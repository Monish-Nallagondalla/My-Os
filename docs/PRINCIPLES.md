# Principles

Version: v0.1.0

---

## 1. Build for Decades

Every decision should still make sense 10 years from now.

Avoid trends.

Build timeless systems.

---

## 2. Think Better, Ship Faster

Every addition must improve either:

- clarity of thought

or

- speed of execution.

Otherwise, don't build it.

---

## 3. Capture Transformations, Not Information

Don't store everything.

Store what changes you.

Lessons.

Decisions.

Frameworks.

Questions.

Experiences that altered your thinking.

---

## 4. AI Assists. Humans Decide.

AI generates.

Humans judge.

Judgment is never delegated.

---

## 5. Build Assets, Not Content

Posts disappear.

Frameworks compound.

Always create reusable knowledge first.

Content is an output.

Assets are the investment.

---

## 6. Questions Before Answers

Better questions create better thinking.

Curiosity is a competitive advantage.

---

## 7. Simplicity Wins

Prefer the smallest system that works.

Complexity must earn its place.

---

## 8. Everything Must Have a Purpose

Every folder.

Every workflow.

Every capability.

Every document.

Every artifact.

Everything earns its place.

---

## 9. Document Decisions

Don't just record what changed.

Record why.

Future understanding depends on today's reasoning.

---

## 10. Evolve Continuously

My-OS is never finished.

It evolves as I evolve.
