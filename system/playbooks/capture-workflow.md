# Capture Workflow

## Trigger

An idea worth remembering.

---

## Step 1

Open `inbox/`

---

## Step 2

Copy `system/templates/idea-capture.md`

---

## Step 3

Rename

```
YYYY-MM-DD-short-title.md
```

Example:

```
2026-07-19-ai-evals.md
```

---

## Step 4

Fill the template in under 2 minutes.

---

## Step 5

Stop.

Do not organize.

Do not improve.

Capture only.
